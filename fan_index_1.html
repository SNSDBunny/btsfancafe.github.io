ABOUT BTS

Oh! You're a new fan of BTS? Well here's a simple background about them.
방탄소년단 (Bangtan Seonyeodan) or also known as BTS or Bulletproof Scouts is a seven-member hip hop group under Big Hit Entertainment consisting of Jin, Suga, J-hope, Rap Monster, Jimin, V and Jungkook. They made their official debut on 12th June 2013 with their First Single "2 Cool 4 Skool" which features their first title track "NO MORE DREAM". Watch their debut music video below!

Their latest music video was FIRE (불타오르네) from their Special Album "화양연화 (The Most Beautiful Moment in Life): Young Forever". For this promotion, they also promote one of the newest track in the latest album called "Save ME". Watch both MVs below:
