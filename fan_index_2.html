Their Biography

BTS/Bangtan Boys/Bulletproof Boy Scouts (also known as 방탄소년단) is a seven-member hip hop group under Big Hit Entertainment consisting of members Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Rap Monster, Jimin, V and Jungkook.

The meaning of their group name Bulletproof Boy Scouts/Bangtan is “to block out the world’s bullets”, and their fanclub name A.R.M.Y stands for “Adorable Representative M.C for Youth”. The group aims to deliver songs that is relatable to their peers, regarding on social problems and giving inspirations to people who are close to their age groups.

The group is known for having talented members, capable of composing, writing and performing their own music. Most notably, rap-line members (Rap Monster, J-hope and Suga) have done several contributions in the making of Bangtan’s albums, production wise and lyric wise. In addition, members from the vocal-line (Jungkook, Jimin, Jin and V) have also involved in the production of their albums as of late.

Members for the group have been scouted for since 2010. Before joining, J-hop was active in the underground dancing scene, participating in various battles and competitions. Rap Monster and SUGA did the same, being active in the underground rapping scene. Before becoming trainees, they focused on their respective interests (dance, singing, acting, rapping), where they eventually passed auditions and ended up in the same group. The original lineup for Bangtan went through several changes before the final group of members was established in 2012.

Half a year prior to debut, the boys began building their name and creating a relationship with fans through Twitter, Vlogs, Fancafe posts, and by releasing various song covers via YouTube and SoundCloud.

Bangtan officially debuted through their showcase on June 12th 2013 with their single album “2COOL4SKOOL.” They promoted ‘No More Dream’ and ‘We Are Bulletproof Pt.2’ on the various music shows . On September 11th 2013 they made their comeback with a 10 track mini-album called “O! RUL8 2?” with their title song ‘N.O’, as well as briefly doing ‘Satoori Rap’. They followed up on November 6th 2013 with ‘The Rise of Bangtan’ promotions. In 2014, they wrapped up their school trilogy series with their 2nd Mini Album "Skool Luv Affair" with their title song "상남자 (Real man)" or the alternative English name "Boy in Luv", and later released their first full-length album "Dark&Wild" which they promoted "Danger" and "War of Hormone".

Later in 2015, they released another trilogy series titled "화양연화" which traslated to "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life". Their 3rd Mini Album titled "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life pt.1" have shot them to stardom locally and internationally with their promotions of their hit single "I Need U" and "DOPE" respectively. Consequently, they launched their first live trilogy concert "BTS Begins" and a world tour following their booming popularity. Later in November, they released their 4th Mini Album "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life pt.2" with their tile track Run which becomes their best-selling album till this day (But the title will be replaced with their upcoming 2nd full length album WINGS that will slay us all into oblivion so #Anticipate). In May 2016, they wrapped up the trilogy with their 3rd installation of the series "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever" which is a special compilation albums that include remixes of their past songs, full version of their Outros and three additional tracks, "불타오르네 (FIRE)", Save Me and EPILOGUE: Young Forever. As part of their special album, they went on an Asian tour known as "BTS 화양연화: EPILOGUE On Stage" from May until early August. Currently, they are gearing up for their 2nd Full Length Album called "WINGS" which will be released on 10th October 2016.

Apart from their musical work, they have featured in various variety shows and have their own shows - Rookie King and BTS American Hustle Life, as well as BTS Gayo, RUN! BTS and BTS Bon Voyage which were brodcasted on Naver's VLIVE and VLIVE+. One of their members, V will also make his acting debut in "Hwarang: The Beginning", a historical drama that will be broadcasted on KBS2 World later in December. They are also active in social medias, especially on Twitter and Youtube where they posted daily selfies and Bangtan Bombs to get closer with ARMYs. BTS is currently the most followed Kpop groups on Twitter in South Korea (currently with 3 million+ followers).
